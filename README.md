This application is a mobile app that is targeted at those interested in turn-based strategy games.

This applications starts in horizontal mode and does not have a vertical mode.
First open the project in Android Studio. Next, run the program.

Once the app has opened, you may press "Tutorial" to view instructions for the game.
Once you are ready to continue, press "Play" and the selection screen will open.

Select three characters and press confirm. The game will now begin.

The enemies are randomly generated on the right side.
On the left side, you may deploy your own troops.
To do so, click onto a tile and press confirm. Repeat until all three are placed.

During Movement Phase, you may press any character and move them to a highlighted tile.
Each character can move up to their designated move range.
Press confirm once every character can no longer be moved or if you are finished moving them.

During Attack Phase, you may press any character and a an enemy within range will be highlighted.
If that enemy is pressed while their highlight is visible, damage will be dealt to them.
Damage dealt can be viewed in the log.
Once an enemy runs out of health, they will be considered defeated and will vanish.
Press confirm after attacking with all characters.

Next, the enemies will have their own turns to move and attack.
The character's healthbars are located underneath their corresponding icons on the left side.
Once they are finished, play automatically returns to the player turn.

This continues until either team is fully defeated.
The player then induces a win or loss state.

*WIN STATE* = All enemies are defeated.
*LOSE STATE* = All player characters are defeated
